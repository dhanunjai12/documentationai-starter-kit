## Setting Preferences

Navigate to the settings panel by clicking on the Goom Social icon and selecting 'Preferences'.

### Key Options

- **Theme**: Switch between light and dark modes.
- **Notification Settings**: Customize alerts to your liking.
- **Chat Personalization**: Tailor responses and interaction style.

<Callout kind="info">Explore different themes to find the one you prefer.</Callout>
